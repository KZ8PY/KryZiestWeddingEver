Frequently Asked Questions

+ How do I get around the city?

Please note that Grab and other ride-sharing apps are not yet available in Puerto Princesa. The most common way to travel is by local tricycle or multicab. We highly recommend coordinating with your hotel for airport transfers or hiring a private van if you are traveling with a group. Car rentals are also a great option if you'd like to explore the city at your own pace.

+ Is there parking at the venues?

Yes! Both our ceremony venue and the reception at the Asturias Hotel have ample parking spaces for guests who plan on driving or renting a vehicle.

+ Can I bring a plus-one or my children?

Due to the intimate seating of our "corner café," we can only accommodate the guests specifically named on your e-vite. While we adore your little ones, we have opted for an invite-only celebration. However, infants who are fully dependent on their parents are more than welcome to join.

+ What time should I arrive?

Our ceremony will begin promptly. We suggest arriving 20–30 minutes early to find a cozy seat, enjoy the music, and settle in before the "first pour" (the start of our ceremony).

+ How do I RSVP, and by when?

To help us finalize our "orders," please RSVP by March 31, 2026. Simply open the sidebar menu and navigate to the RSVP page to let us know if you’ll be joining us!

+ Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We've hired a wonderful team to capture our "God-written journey," so we kindly ask that you keep your phones and cameras tucked away during the ceremony. We want to see your faces, not your devices! Once we finish our "I Dos", feel free to snap and share as many "matchical" memories as you'd like.
